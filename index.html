Education

I majored in Physics with minors in both Mathematics and Computer Science at the University of Pittsburgh, and graduated with a Bachelors of Science w/ Honors in April, 2012.

My major curriculum consisted of 3 courses in Newtonian Mechanics, 3 courses in Electricity and Magnetism, 2 courses in Quantum Mechanics, 1 course in Statistical Mechanics/Thermodynamics, 2 courses in Modern Physics, 1 course in Computational Physics, and labs in Optics, Digital/Analog Electronics, and Modern Physics.

The Math minor took me up through Calculus 1-3, Differential Equations, Linear Algebra, Complex Variable Analysis, Vector/Tensor Analysis, and Differential Geometry. The Computer Science minor required Intermediate Programming in Java, Introduction to Assembly Language/Digital Logic, and Data Structures.

I graduated with 141 credits, and what I feel to be a very well-rounded skillset. I'm convinced I can learn anything, and I have yet to be proven wrong about that.
